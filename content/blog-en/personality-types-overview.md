# The Complete Guide to 8 Night Personality Types

The Night Personality Test identifies 8 personality types based on 3 core axes. Each type has its own unique appeal and characteristics.

## The 3 Diagnostic Axes

### 1. Lead (L) vs. Follow (F)

**Lead types (L)** prefer to take the initiative and find joy in guiding their partner.

**Follow types (F)** feel most comfortable going along with their partner and value a receptive approach.

### 2. Adventurous (A) vs. Stable (S)

**Adventurous types (A)** prefer bold, dynamic approaches.

**Stable types (S)** value taking time to build deep, meaningful connections.

### 3. Free (F) vs. Devoted (L)

**Free types (F)** seek open, liberated relationships.

**Devoted types (L)** prioritize deep bonds and stable connections.

## Why Knowing Your Type Matters

Understanding your personality type offers several benefits:

- **Deeper self-awareness**: You can objectively grasp your behavioral patterns and tendencies
- **Better compatibility understanding**: Gain insights into your compatibility with partners and how to build stronger relationships
- **Improved communication**: Discover ways to connect that play to your natural strengths
