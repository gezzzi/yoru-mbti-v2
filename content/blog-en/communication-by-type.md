# How to Delight Your Partner: Communication Tips by Type

Once you know your partner's personality type, try **communicating in a way that matches their type**. This article covers effective approaches for each of the 8 types.

## Approaching Leader Types

### LAL -- The Passionate Leader

**What makes them happy:**
- Being relied upon
- Having their leadership recognized
- Having their passion received and accepted

**Effective Communication:**

| Situation | Example Phrases |
|-----------|----------------|
| Complimenting | "I want to follow your lead" / "You're so dependable" |
| Being affectionate | "Take charge" / "I'll leave it to you" |
| Showing gratitude | "I feel so safe with you" |

**What to avoid:**
- Trying to take control from them
- Showing indifference
- Dismissing their feelings

---

### LAF -- The Wild Maverick

**What makes them happy:**
- Having their freedom respected
- Enjoying new things together
- Not being tied down

**Effective Communication:**

| Situation | Example Phrases |
|-----------|----------------|
| Complimenting | "Being with you is so fun" / "You're exciting" |
| Suggesting something | "I found something interesting" / "Want to try it?" |
| Giving space | "Make sure to take time for yourself too" |

**What to avoid:**
- Being clingy or controlling
- Restricting their actions
- Only having heavy conversations

---

### LSL -- The Devoted Protector

**What makes them happy:**
- Being thanked for their efforts
- Having their partner lean on them
- Feeling truly valued

**Effective Communication:**

| Situation | Example Phrases |
|-----------|----------------|
| Showing gratitude | "Thank you, that made me so happy" / "You noticed!" |
| Leaning on them | "Could you do this for me?" / "Can I count on you?" |
| Expressing love | "I couldn't manage without you" |

**What to avoid:**
- Taking their efforts for granted
- Forgetting to say thank you
- Rejecting their help

---

### LSF -- The Cool Strategist

**What makes them happy:**
- Having their abilities recognized
- Efficient communication
- Not having their rhythm disrupted

**Effective Communication:**

| Situation | Example Phrases |
|-----------|----------------|
| Complimenting | "Impressive" / "That's great" (keep it simple) |
| Consulting them | "What do you think?" (ask logically) |
| Expressing love | Show it through actions rather than words |

**What to avoid:**
- Being overly emotional
- Talking at length
- Forcing inefficient activities

---

## Approaching Supporter Types

### FAL -- The Romantic Enthusiast

**What makes them happy:**
- Getting excited together
- Hearing love expressed in words
- Having their loneliness eased

**Effective Communication:**

| Situation | Example Phrases |
|-----------|----------------|
| Complimenting | "Being with you is so fun" / "I want more time with you" |
| Reassuring | "I'll always be by your side" / "I love you so much" |
| Energizing | Respond with high energy and enthusiasm |

**What to avoid:**
- Ignoring them
- Cold attitudes
- Being stingy with affection

---

### FAF -- The Free Spirit

**What makes them happy:**
- Enjoying the present moment
- Matching energy and vibe
- Keeping things relaxed

**Effective Communication:**

| Situation | Example Phrases |
|-----------|----------------|
| Suggesting something | "Want to do something right now?" (keep it light) |
| Having fun | Laugh and be playful together |
| Managing distance | Maintain an appropriate level of lightness |

**What to avoid:**
- Heavy conversations that drag on
- Pressuring with future plans
- Being too serious

---

### FSL -- The Tender Soul

**What makes them happy:**
- Feeling secure and safe
- Being treated as special
- Spending lots of time together

**Effective Communication:**

| Situation | Example Phrases |
|-----------|----------------|
| Reassuring | "You're the only one" / "Let's stay together always" |
| Expressing love | Stay in touch often; plenty of physical affection |
| Complimenting | "You're adorable" / "You're so precious to me" |

**What to avoid:**
- Leaving messages on read
- Talking about others too much
- Being too distant

---

### FSF -- The Easy-Going Charmer

**What makes them happy:**
- Having their pace respected
- Spending calm, peaceful time together
- Subtle gestures of affection

**Effective Communication:**

| Situation | Example Phrases |
|-----------|----------------|
| Complimenting | Casually and naturally |
| Spending time together | No need to force excitement |
| Expressing love | Gentle, without being pushy |

**What to avoid:**
- Rushing them
- Demanding high energy
- Disrupting their rhythm

## Universal Tips for All Types

### 1. Do Not Over-Stereotype

Types show tendencies, not absolutes. **Every person is unique** -- never forget that.

### 2. Take Care of Yourself Too

Do not lose yourself trying to match your partner. **Balance** is essential.

### 3. Confirm with Words

"Is this right?" "How did that feel?" **Checking in with your partner** matters.

### 4. Notice Changes

Your partner's type may shift over time. Always pay attention to **who they are now**.

## When You Do Not Know Their Type

### Things to Observe

1. **Lead or Follow**: Who makes decisions? Do they want to be in control?
2. **Adventurous or Stable**: Is their pace fast? Or slow and careful?
3. **Free or Devoted**: Do they resist commitment? Or seek deep bonds?

### Just Ask

The most reliable method is to **take the test together**.

"There's this fun personality test -- want to try it with me?"

## Summary

Tailoring your communication to your partner's type can make your relationship much stronger.

**Key Points:**
- Identify what makes your partner's type happy
- Be mindful of what to avoid
- Do not stereotype -- really see your partner
- Take care of yourself too
