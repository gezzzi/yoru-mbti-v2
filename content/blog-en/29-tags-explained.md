# Understanding the 29 Tags and Your Unique Combination

In addition to the 8 personality types, the Night Personality Test uses **29 different tags** to paint a more detailed picture of who you are. This article explains each tag and how to interpret your combination.

## What Are Tags?

Tags express your personality and tendencies in **finer detail**.

- Personality type: Your broad tendencies
- Tags: Your specific characteristics

Each person's combination of tags is different, creating **a profile that is uniquely yours**.

## Tags by Category

### Passion and Desire

#### Flame of Desire
**Meaning:** Someone with irrepressible desires

People with this tag are honest about their desires and do not try to hide them. They are passionate, and that heat easily reaches their partner.

#### Kiss Enthusiast
**Meaning:** Someone who ignites feelings and passion through kissing

A type that places special importance on kissing. They find deep meaning in the kiss itself, not just as a prelude.

### Communication

#### Verbal Play
**Meaning:** Gets excited through teasing words and conversation

A type that values verbal communication. Words and conversation are key elements in building excitement.

#### Open Talker
**Meaning:** Comfortable with risque conversation

Prefers open communication and can talk without taboos.

### Play Style

#### Roleplay Fan
**Meaning:** Enjoys getting into character and scenario play

A type that enjoys creating situations. They find excitement in playing roles and stepping into different worlds.

#### Cosplay Lover
**Meaning:** Gets excited by uniforms and costumes

A type that values visual stimulation. They enjoy transformation through outfits and appearances.

#### Mirror Play
**Meaning:** Finds excitement in watching themselves or their partner in the mirror

A type that seeks visual excitement through a more objective perspective.

#### Gamification
**Meaning:** Enjoys rules, missions, and playful elements

A type that values a sense of fun. They prefer to enjoy things with a game-like approach.

### Tempo and Style

#### Quick Starter
**Meaning:** Prefers getting straight to the point

A type that prefers a fast pace. They favor a direct approach over lengthy buildup.

#### Impulse-Driven
**Meaning:** Acts on the spur of the moment

A type that values impulse over planning. They are honest about their feelings in the moment.

#### Slow and Steady
**Meaning:** Prefers a gentle, unhurried pace

A type that prefers a slow tempo. They find satisfaction in gentle stimulation.

#### Repeat Seeker
**Meaning:** Does not stop at just once

A type with stamina. They want to go again rather than stopping after one round.

### Atmosphere and Ambiance

#### Romance First
**Meaning:** Values mood-setting and atmosphere

A type that prioritizes the vibe. They care about lighting, music, fragrance, and other elements that set the mood.

#### Aftercare Essential
**Meaning:** Values cuddling and conversation afterward

A type that cherishes the time after intimacy. They cannot feel satisfied without aftercare.

### Exploration and Challenge

#### Pioneer
**Meaning:** Pursues unknown pleasures

A type that wants to try new things. They are always seeking new stimulation.

#### Light BDSM Tolerant
**Meaning:** Open to light restraint and pain play

A type that can accept slightly harder stimulation.

#### Research Enthusiast
**Meaning:** Enjoys learning from stories and gathering information

A knowledge-seeking type. They want to improve themselves through learning.

### Special Interests

#### Voyeur/Exhibitionist
**Meaning:** Thrilled by watching or being watched

A type that feels excitement from gazes. They have special feelings about being seen or seeing.

#### Morning Person
**Meaning:** More energized in the morning or daytime

A type that is at their best during the morning or daytime rather than at night.

### Digital

#### Digital Foreplay
**Meaning:** Gets excited through chat, calls, or content

A type that can build excitement through digital communication.

### Partner Approach

#### Guide
**Meaning:** Supports and leads their partner

A type that prefers to lead their partner. They find joy in teaching and guiding.

#### Multitasker
**Meaning:** Skilled at using hands, mouth, and accessories simultaneously

A dexterous type. They can do multiple things at once.

### Boundaries and Safety

#### Clear Boundaries
**Meaning:** Good at communicating limits and boundaries

A type that can clearly express their limits. They are skilled communicators.

#### Hygiene Focused
**Meaning:** Values cleanliness, preparation, and clean-up

A type that prioritizes hygiene. Cleanliness brings them a sense of security.

#### Safety First
**Meaning:** Prioritizes safety and security above all

A risk-averse type. Being safe is the most important thing.

## Reading Your Tag Combination

### Combination Examples

**Example 1: Flame of Desire + Slow and Steady**
> Strong passion, but at a gentle pace. A type that takes their time to savor deeply.

**Example 2: Quick Starter + Aftercare Essential**
> Gets right to the action, but is thorough with aftercare afterward. A type that knows contrast.

**Example 3: Roleplay Fan + Romance First**
> Values both atmosphere and scenario. A true director type.

### Making the Most of Your Tags

1. **Leverage your strengths**: Your tags are your individuality. Use them as your signature.
2. **Share with your partner**: Tags make it easier to communicate your preferences.
3. **Check compatibility**: Compare your tags with your partner's to gauge how well you match.

## Summary

The 29 tags express your individuality in fine detail.

- Knowing your tags deepens self-understanding
- Everyone's tag combination is different
- Sharing tags with your partner makes communication smoother
