# Lead vs. Follow Types: A Complete Breakdown

One of the most important axes in the Night Personality Test is "Lead (L)" vs. "Follow (F)." This article takes a deep look at the characteristics of each.

## Lead Type (L) Characteristics

### Core Personality

Lead types are those who **feel most comfortable taking the initiative**.

- Naturally good at making the first move
- Find joy in guiding their partner
- Like to plan and execute
- Have a strong sense of responsibility and thrive when relied upon

### The Appeal of Lead Types

1. **Reassuring presence**: They provide direction when things are uncertain
2. **Decisiveness**: They keep things moving smoothly without hesitation
3. **Proactive nature**: They take action rather than just waiting

### What Lead Types Should Watch Out For

- Leave room to hear your partner's opinions
- Be careful not to become self-centered
- Try letting your partner take the lead sometimes

## Follow Type (F) Characteristics

### Core Personality

Follow types are those who **feel most comfortable going along with their partner**.

- Skilled at reading their partner's feelings
- Receptive and accepting
- Flexible and adaptable to situations
- Find happiness in seeing their partner's joy

### The Appeal of Follow Types

1. **Embrace**: They respect their partner's pace and provide comfort
2. **Empathy**: They can truly connect with their partner's feelings
3. **Flexibility**: They harmonize well with anyone

### What Follow Types Should Watch Out For

- Value your own opinions too
- Avoid settling for "anything is fine" too often
- Try making suggestions on your own sometimes

## Lead x Follow Compatibility

### The Ideal Complementary Relationship

Lead and Follow types are the most naturally compatible pairing.

- The Lead type sets the direction; the Follow type provides support
- Clear roles for each person reduce stress
- The relationship progresses with a natural rhythm

### When Both Are the Same Type

**Lead x Lead:**
- Power struggles can easily arise
- Both need to practice stepping back
- Focus on "cooperation" rather than "competition"

**Follow x Follow:**
- Both tend to wait for the other
- Sometimes you need the courage to take the first step
- Start with small suggestions

## Making the Most of Your Type

### For Lead Types

If your partner is a Follow type, feel confident leading. However, always watch their reactions and adjust your pace accordingly.

If your partner is also a Lead type, try saying "I'll leave it to you today" sometimes. A relationship where both can lead is actually quite stimulating.

### For Follow Types

If your partner is a Lead type, feel free to go with the flow. But never forget to voice what you dislike or what you want.

If your partner is also a Follow type, summon a little courage. A single "How about we try this?" can be the spark that moves the relationship forward.
